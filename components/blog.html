Description For Laravel Blog Page

Home Page

  • Accessible to all users
  • Displays list of articles with titles and descriptions
  • Allows viewing article details and comments
  • Provides filtering and sorting options

Articles Management

  • Registered users can add articles
  • Includes fields for title, content, and details

Comments

  • Requires login or sign up to leave comments
  • Users can delete their own comments
  • Article owners can delete comments on their articles
